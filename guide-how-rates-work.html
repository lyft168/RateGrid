How Exchange Rates Work /guides/how-exchange-rates-work/

Editorial hub page — builds topical authority and links down to data pages.

An exchange rate is the price of one currency in another. 1 SGD = 0.7746 USD is the mid-market rate — the midpoint banks trade at.

Rates move on interest rates, inflation and sentiment. The charts on each pair page (SGD → USD, SGD → EUR, SGD → JPY) show 1-month to 5-year trends.

Hub-and-spoke: this guide links down to rate pages, each rate page links back up. That internal web is most of why content-led currency sites rank — and it's what keeps the site from looking like a thin, auto-generated farm.
